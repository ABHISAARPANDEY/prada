# Founders Section - Layman's Guide

## What is This Section?

The Founders section tells you about the people who built and led PRADA. It's like a "Hall of Fame" showing the visionaries who transformed PRADA from a small shop into a global luxury empire. This section shows you the human story behind the numbers.

## What Information Does It Show?

### For Each Founder/Leader:
- **Name**: Who they are
- **Role**: What position they held (Founder, CEO, Creative Director)
- **Period**: When they were active (e.g., 1913-1958, 1978-Present)
- **Description**: Their story and contribution
- **Achievements**: Key things they accomplished
- **Image**: Photo of the person

### The Three Key Figures:
1. **Mario Prada** (1913-1958): The original founder who started it all
2. **Miuccia Prada** (1978-Present): The creative genius who modernized the brand
3. **Patrizio Bertelli** (1977-Present): The business strategist who expanded globally

## Why Is This Important?

Understanding the founders helps you understand:
- **Company Culture**: The values and vision that shaped PRADA
- **Strategic Decisions**: Why PRADA made certain business choices
- **Brand Identity**: How PRADA became known for intellectual, avant-garde design
- **Growth Story**: The journey from small shop to global brand

## Common Questions You Might Be Asked

1. **"Who started PRADA?"**
   - Mario Prada started PRADA in 1913 as a small leather goods shop in Milan, Italy.

2. **"Who runs PRADA now?"**
   - Miuccia Prada (granddaughter of Mario) is the Creative Director, and Patrizio Bertelli is the CEO. They work together as co-CEOs.

3. **"What did Miuccia Prada do?"**
   - She transformed PRADA from a traditional leather goods company into a global fashion powerhouse. She's known for intellectual, minimalist designs that challenge conventional luxury fashion.

4. **"How did PRADA become so successful?"**
   - Through the combination of Miuccia's creative vision and Patrizio's business strategy, PRADA expanded globally, went public, and became one of the world's leading luxury brands.

5. **"What's the PRADA legacy timeline?"**
   - The timeline shows key milestones: 1913 (founded), 1978 (Miuccia takes over), 1984 (iconic nylon backpack), 1993 (Miu Miu brand), 2011 (IPO), and recent financial success.

## Real-World Analogy

Think of founders like the parents of a family business. Just like you'd want to know about a family's history to understand their values and traditions, knowing about PRADA's founders helps you understand the company's DNA and why they make certain decisions.

## Key Takeaways

- Mario Prada founded the company in 1913
- Miuccia Prada (his granddaughter) revolutionized the brand starting in 1978
- Patrizio Bertelli brought business strategy and global expansion
- The combination of creative vision and business acumen led to success

When presenting:
1. Use founder stories to explain company values and brand positioning

3. Show how leadership continuity contributed to consistent growth
4. Use the timeline to show PRADA's evolution over 110+ years
